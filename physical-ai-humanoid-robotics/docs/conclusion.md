## The Physical AI Paradigm

### What Makes Physical AI Special

Physical AI differs from traditional AI in several fundamental ways:

#### Embodied Cognition
- Intelligence emerges from the interaction between brain, body, and environment
- Physical constraints shape cognitive processes
- Sensorimotor coordination is essential for intelligent behavior

#### Real-World Interaction
- Continuous interaction with dynamic, unpredictable environments
- Real-time processing requirements
- Physical safety and reliability concerns

#### Human Compatibility
- Natural interaction modalities
- Social intelligence and norms
- Emotional and cognitive compatibility

### The Humanoid Advantage

Humanoid robots offer unique advantages:
- **Environment Compatibility**: Designed for human environments
- **Interaction Naturalness**: Familiar form factor for human interaction
- **Capability Generality**: Human-like dexterity and mobility
- **Social Acceptance**: More readily accepted by humans
