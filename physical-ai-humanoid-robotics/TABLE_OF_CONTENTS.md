## Learning Pathways

### For Beginners
Start with Modules 1-2 to build foundational knowledge in ROS 2 and simulation before progressing to more advanced topics.

### For Practitioners
Focus on Modules 3-4 for practical implementation of AI-powered humanoid robots, with optional deep dives into specialized applications.

### For Researchers
Emphasize Modules 6-7 for advanced control techniques and ethical considerations, supplemented by current literature and research papers.

### For Educators
Use the modular structure to create customized curricula, with labs and assessments provided for each module.
